# Lens: Key Vocab & Grammar

Apply when the input contains words, collocations, or structures worth learning. Tailor depth to `profile.md`: go deep on weak spots, stay light on mastered areas.

## What to produce
**Key Vocab & Grammar（重点词汇与语法）**
- 2–5 items: hard words, collocations, fixed phrases, or notable grammar/structure.
- For each: brief target-language gloss + anchor-language explanation + why it matters (register, precision, idiom).
- Mark any item already in `profile.md` as **review** and any new one as **new** (eligible for future resurfacing).

## Hand-off
For a single word/term the user wants drilled in full (IPA, all senses, mnemonics), defer to the **vocab** skill rather than duplicating it here.

## Principles
- Pick items that unlock the sentence, not every unfamiliar word.
- Connect to spaced repetition: surface a due **review** item from `profile.md` when relevant.

## Example
"leverage this opportunity to get ahead" →
- *leverage* (v.) **new** — 善用、借力；比 use 更专业，商务常用。
- *get ahead of* — 抢先、领先于；固定搭配。
